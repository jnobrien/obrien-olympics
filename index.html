Boggle

Materials:

  • Big boggle game
  • Pencils
  • Paper

Instructions:

  1. Start timer and find as many words as you can before the timer runs out.
  2. Words must be at least 4 letters long.
  3. Pencils down when the timer runs out.

Scoring: One point per unique word (must be a legitimate word). Points are awarded in order of most number of unique words.

Candy Cane Fishing

Materials:

  • Mason jar
  • Plate
  • Can to weigh down the plate
  • Red Solo cup
  • Measuring tape
  • Seven candy canes
  • Stopwatch

Instructions:

  1. Set up the candy cane pond. Turn the Mason jar upside down and center the plate on top. Put the can on top of the plate to keep it stable.
  2. Hang six of the candy canes from the plate, evenly spaced around the edges.
  3. Use the measuring tape to place the red Solo cup two feet from the edge of the Mason jar.
  4. Unwrap the straight end of the remaining candy cane and place it in your mouth with the hook side out.
  5. Start the stopwatch.
  6. Use the hook of the candy in your mouth to move the candy canes from the candy cane pond to the red Solo cup. You may not use your hands or anything other than the candy cane in your mouth.
  7. If you accidentally knock over the red Solo cup, you may use your hands to set it upright again with the candy canes inside.
  8. Stop the stopwatch when you've collected all six candy canes in the red Solo cup.
  9. Reset the pond and repeat the process for your partner.

Scoring: Add partner times together. Points are awarded in order of quickest total time.

Candy Cane Twirling Marathon

Materials:

  • One candy cane per person (plus one extra in case it breaks during the practice time)
  • Timer
  • Stopwatch

Instructions:

  1. Hang your candy cane from one finger - you may choose which finger to use.
  2. Set a thirty second timer for a practice round.
  3. Start the timer and take thirty seconds to practice spinning the candy cane around your finger.
  4. Once the practice round has ended, reset the candy cane on your finger. Replace the candy cane if it fell and broke during the practice round.
  5. Have you partner start a stopwatch.
  6. Spin the candy cand around your finger.
  7. Stop the stopwatch when your candy cane falls or stops spinning.
  8. Repeat the process for your partner.

Scoring: Add partner times together. Points are awarded in order of longest total time.

Candy Cane Board

Materials:

  • Large colorful candy cane
  • Five big marshmallows per person
  • Candy cane board (designed by Mom)
  • Tape

Instructions:

  1. Set up the Candy Cane board on a flat non-carpeted surface and use tape to secure the corners.
  2. Place a marshmallow behind the starting line.
  3. Use either end of your candy cane to push/flick/hit your marshmallow down the Candy Cane Board.
  4. Take two practice hits before resetting your marshmallows.
  5. Once you are ready to start, push/flick/hit all five of your marshmallows one at a time toward the lines. Do NOT move the marshmallows you've already hit - this means you can hit your own marshmallow with another marshmallow, either scoring more points or knocking it out of the point area.
  6. After you've hit all five marshmallows, count up your points.
  7. Clear the board before your partner takes their turn.

Scoring: Add partner scores together. Points are awarded in order of highest scores.

Peppermint Skee-Ball

Materials:

  • Two red Solo cups
  • One red Solo cup with a ribbon tied around
  • Ten peppermint balls
  • Measuring tape
  • Painter's tape

Instructions:

  1. Set up the play area by making two lines on the ground with painter's tape that are six feet apart.
  2. Set up one partner with one red Solo cup tied around their waist and a red Solo cup in each hand.
  3. Stand across from your partner, each behind opposite sides of the tape.
  4. Keeping your toes behind the line, gently toss one of the peppermint balls at the red Solo cups your partner is holding.
  5. The partner holding the red Solo cups may move as they wish to catch the peppermint balls, as long as they stay behind their taped line.
  6. Repeat with all ten peppermint balls.
  7. Count your points. One point for each peppermint ball in the red Solo cups in your hand and 3 points for each peppermint ball in the red Solo cup tied around your waist.
  8. Switch roles and repeat with the other partner throwing.

Scoring: Add points together. Points are awarded in order of highest scores.

Candy Cane Spin

Materials:

  • Large colorful candy cane
  • Four Hershey's kisses
  • Candy Cane Spin board (designed by Mom)
  • Stopwatch

Instructions:

  1. Set up the Candy Cane Spin board on a flat surface.
  2. Set one Hershey's kiss in each quadrant, leaving enough room to spin your candy cane without hitting a Hershey's kiss.
  3. Place the large candy cane on the X in the middle of the board.
  4. Start your stopwatch.
  5. Spin the candy cane on the board - it's okay if it moves off of the X.
  6. Take the Hershey's kiss from whichever quadrant the straight end of the candy cane is pointing to. If you've already collected the Hershey's kiss from that quadrant, spin again.
  7. Reset the candy cane on the X before every spin.
  8. Stop the stopwatch when you've collected all four Hershey's kisses.

Scoring: Add partner times together. Points are awarded in order of shortest time.
